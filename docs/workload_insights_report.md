# Workload and Learning Correlation Report (Synthetic)

This document is fully synthetic and created only for hackathon demonstration.

Insights:
- Employees with more than 20 meeting hours per week may struggle to complete study plans.
- Strong completion is more likely when learners have at least 15 focus hours per week.
- Morning learning slots are useful for learners with heavy afternoon meetings.
- Evening learning slots should be used carefully because fatigue can reduce consistency.

Recommendation:
Schedule learning blocks during focus-heavy periods and reduce weekly study targets for learners with high meeting load.
