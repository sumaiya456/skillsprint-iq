# Engineering Certification Enablement Guide (Synthetic)

This document is fully synthetic and created only for hackathon demonstration.

## Role to Certification Mapping

Cloud Engineer:
- Primary certification: AZ-204
- Secondary certification: AZ-305
- Recommended preparation pattern: 1 to 2 hours of focused learning per day.

DevOps Engineer:
- Primary certification: AZ-400
- Recommended preparation pattern: weekly hands-on practice and CI/CD labs.

Data Engineer:
- Primary certification: DP-203
- Recommended preparation pattern: scenario-based practice using data pipelines and monitoring cases.

AI Engineer:
- Primary certification: AI-102
- Recommended preparation pattern: applied exercises covering AI services, prompt flow, evaluation, and Responsible AI.

## General Readiness Rule

A learner should reach at least 75% practice score before attempting most certification exams.
For AI-102, a target practice score of 78% is recommended because the scenario questions require stronger applied reasoning.
