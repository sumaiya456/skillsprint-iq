# Certification Readiness Rules (Synthetic)

This document is fully synthetic and created only for hackathon demonstration.

## Readiness Status

Ready:
- Practice score is equal to or above the certification target.
- Hours studied meet or exceed recommended hours.
- Pending modules are low.

Nearly Ready:
- Practice score is within 5 points of the target.
- Hours studied are close to the recommended hours.
- Learner needs final revision or one mock assessment.

At Risk:
- Practice score is below target by more than 5 points.
- Hours studied are below recommended hours.
- Workload signals show low focus time or high meeting load.

## Manager Escalation Rule

Escalate to manager insight when:
- A team has two or more at-risk learners.
- A learner has meeting hours above 20 and focus hours below 12.
- A learner is below target score and has pending core modules.
